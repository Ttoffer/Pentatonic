iPhone & iPad

For the best experience and to add an icon to your home screen, use Safari (not Chrome or another browser for this step).

  1. Open Safari and go to https://ttoffer.github.io/Pentatonic/
  2. Tap the Share button (square with an arrow).
  3. Scroll and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. Edit the name if you like, then tap Add.

You’ll get an icon that opens without Safari’s address bar, like a small app. Repeat on each device you use.

Tip: If the site or icon updates and something looks old, remove the home screen shortcut and add it again from Safari.
